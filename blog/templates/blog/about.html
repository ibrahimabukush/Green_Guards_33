About Us

Welcome to Green Guards, your digital platform for environmental activism and community engagement. At Green Guards, we believe in the power of collective action to create positive change for our planet..

We're dedicated to protecting our environment by providing a platform for you to report environmental issues directly to your local municipality. Simply snap a photo, upload it with a brief description and your location, and we'll ensure it reaches the right authorities for action. Join us in safeguarding our planet for future generations. Together, let's make a difference, one report at a time. Thank you for being a part of the Green Guards community. Let's guard our green!
